ABOUT CAREGO

CareGo is a technology business based in Burlington, Ontario. The company provides products and services
that enable manufacturers such as steel mills, steel service centers and pipe and tube mills, utilize
automation to move, store and retrieve their heavy products (steel coils, plate, slabs, sheets, pipes
and tubes) with unparalleled ease, safety and efficiency.
